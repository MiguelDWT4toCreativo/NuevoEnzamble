Customized
Outdoor Kitchens

The kitchen has always been an intimate and personal space where we enjoy the company of friends and family and we all have our own ideas about how this space should be. DFN offers a dedicated design service to help you create your perfect kitchen, even outdoors.

Our technical staff work closely with customers throughout the entire process and are always ready to respond to your requests, however complex they may be.

Luxury curved kitchen

special edition with automatic cover and doors in micro mosaic of glass

This isn’t just a mere kitchen, it’s a real gem. Personality and functionality declined in the uniqueness of this new model. The search for one-of-a-kind solutions has resulted in Samuele Mazza coming up with this new creation which once again represents a perfect mix of shapes, original finishes and cutting-edge technology. While fully respecting all the functional features which a kitchen must have, all customizable in terms of details and accessories, what makes this model stand out is its design, which further refines the idea of open-air kitchen.

Next to the rounded sides and the automatic sliding cover, made of the same material as the top, what strikes most is the door decoration which consists of a glass micromosaic covering that forms dreamlike patterns with iridescent reflections and a design representing a real glass tapestry. The double-sided kitchen is conceived to be accessed from both sides: by sliding the top, on one side, a fully-equipped work surface is revealed, while, on the other, a convenient snack table comes into view.

Our tools and services for you

We will support you from the initial design through to installation on site, bringing to life your custom kitchen design. Work with us to create your perfect outdoor kitchen: tell us which model you prefer and what your requirements are in terms of style, space, layout of appliances and accessories. Our team will put together a design tailored to your needs.

Below, you will find all the necessary information about the company and the services we offer with the aim of ensuring a complete and valuable end product.

Our designers

Our designers are here to help you at every stage of the process. Not knowing where to start when designing an outdoor kitchen can be daunting, which is why we have a dedicated team in DFN to provide support throughout the design process.

We have the experience of those who have designed numerous outdoor kitchens, and we have accumulated years of valuable knowledge of the sector when it comes to designing beautiful and functional outdoor spaces. Our professional outdoor kitchen designers can support you at every stage of the journey, offering you the help you need to turn a project vision into a perfect plan.

Our design services

Our design experts have studied many environments, from modest spaces to large outdoor kitchens with a series of complementary accessories. The DFN team knows how to build an outdoor kitchen to fit any vision and has solved more design problems than you can imagine. We can provide support in all aspects including:

– Selection of the best appliances to offer the desired kitchen and fun experiences;
– Organisation of equipment and preparation areas that allow the mostcomfortable workflows;
– Answer the questions about the preparation of the area that is to host the kitchen;
– Work within space or budget limits;
– Provide CAD drawings and renderings to help visualise the project and facilitate installation.

Our design process

1. Define the space;
2. Select the products you can’t go without in the outdoor kitchen;
3. Choose all the finishes that fit your style
4. Consult our renderings and finalise your plan;
5. Choose and proceed with the order.

The ordering proccess

Our team of experts accompanies you at every stage of the process. Once you have selected the necessities, the installation space and the finishes, your design consultant will create a formal quote that includes everything that contributes to creating the kitchen of your dreams.

Our team will create CAD drawings and renderings of your personalised kitchen so that the final aspect will be clear. The drawings include all dimensions as well as gas, plumbing and power supply attachments. Once the kitchen has been finalised and all the choices made in terms of finishes, DFN proceeds with the production of the kitchen, which can be shipped anywhere in the world, pre-assembled and accompanied by all the necessary installation instructions.
